About Our Games

Hit and Run you play as a dog who must avoid getting hit by on coming police cars who wish to destroy you Your goal for this game is to NOT GET HIT!!
In Paper Chaser you play as Mr.Crab of Binkini Bottom a money hungry crab; Your goal is to get all the money without dying but beware being money hungry may cost you your life.
Game Control:up,down,left,and right arrow keys also in Hit and Run use space bar to launch special feature
