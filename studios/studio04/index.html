A Little Info

For "DON'T CATCH FEELINGS", you use th arrow keys to move around and the space key to shoot those feelings of love and compassion down. Don't catch feelings now. You've come too far.

For "Adventures of Doodle Bop", use the arrow keys up, left, and right to move around. Collect items such as hearts, jewels, and DINERO-which means $money$- and remember to find the key to leave. Get to the majical door portal and GET DOODLE BOP HOME.
